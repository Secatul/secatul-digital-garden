Hello friends, it’s been a while…

It’s been a few months since I last posted here because I made a huge decision in my career: switching from software development to cybersecurity.

---

### 📚 What I started with

Here’s all the material I gathered to begin:

- [HTB Academy: Junior Analyst](https://academy.hackthebox.com/path/preview/junior-cybersecurity-analyst)  
- [TryHackMe: Pre-Security](https://tryhackme.com/path/outline/presecurity) (completed ✅)  
- [TryHackMe: Cybersecurity 101](https://tryhackme.com/path/outline/cybersecurity101) (currently doing it)  
- [CCNA 1 (NetAcad)](https://www.netacad.com/courses/networking/ccna-introduction-networks)

I decided to start with TryHackMe because people said it’s more beginner-friendly — and they were right.  
I really liked the content. It’s very hands-on and covers many topics: defensive and offensive security, networking, Windows, Linux, and how the web works.  

I already knew a few things from my Computer Science degree (I’m graduating this February 🎓), but a lot of the content was new to me, which made learning exciting.  
And yes — I got the Pre-Security certificate!

### 🔍 Going deeper

After that, I began studying **HTB Junior Analyst** and **TryHackMe Cybersecurity 101** simultaneously.  
Both have similar goals and topics. I found HTB’s explanations clearer, but THM’s hands-on approach felt even better.

Still, I quickly realized that learning Python and Linux only through THM and HTB wouldn’t be enough.  
So I decided to split my study plan a bit.

For **Linux**, I started reading *Linux for Hackers* — great book so far.  
To practice, I’m doing [OverTheWire](https://overthewire.org/warggames/), [LabEx](https://labex.io), and [CMD Challenge](https://cmdchallenge.com/), which are helping me a lot with real commands.

For **Python**, I began following a tutorial but felt like I needed more structure, so I started *Automate the Boring Stuff with Python*.  
LabEx also has Python labs, and I recently found [Exercism](https://exercism.org), which I’ll start soon.
